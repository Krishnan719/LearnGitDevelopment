Open Gmail Application

1. Open the chrome browser

2. Enter the gmail url

3. Enter the username, password

4. Click the login button.

5. Able to see the mails

6. Click the Compose button a new mail can be created

7. Open the mail click the Reply option and send it

8. Open the mail click the Forward option and send it

9. Ensure the Reply All functionality is implemented and verify the same

10. Select the check box to any of the email and click the delete icon ensure the email is deleted.
